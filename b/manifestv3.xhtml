Manifest V3

Chrome is moving to the manifest V3 extension system in 2024 which basically kills adblocker extensions.

Now this is not surprising, Google has the biggest ad network, a huge amount of their revenue comes from ads.

Recently they even made YouTube unusable if you had an adblocker.

If you want to stop the chromium monopoly please use Firefox.

Also some advice when you switch to Firefox:

  • Get uBlock Origin or uMatrix (if you want things to just werk, get uBlock Origin)
  • Get rid of Mozilla telemetry via user.js
  • Avoid Firefox forks that claim to have better privacy. These forks are not always up-to-date and you can just achieve the same effect with a good user.js

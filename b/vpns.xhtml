"Best" VPN

Most people don't need a VPN, here is why:

  • Your connection is already encrypted and secure on websites because of HTTPS (yes even on public Wi-Fi), you don't need a VPN for that
  • Most VPNs are horrible for privacy, you give out all your info to a random company when you subscribe to a VPN service
  • Using a VPN is not enough to be anonym online, browser fingerprinting can deanonymize you even with a VPN

Instead of VPNs, you should use decentralized technologies like TOR and I2P to mask your IP.

If you really need a VPN, get Mullvad, since they don't ask for any personal info and you can pay privately.

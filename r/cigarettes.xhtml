Cigarettes

Marlboro Menthol 100's

Tier: S

Great menthol taste, nice firm filter, burns for a long time and the right amount of nicotine. This is my fav for sure.

Camel Blue

Tier: S

The best non-menthol cigarette I have ever had. Its smooth, doesn't have a chemical taste, burns well and has the right amount of nicotine for me.

Marlboro Mega Ice Blast

Tier: A

Smooth menthol taste once the capsule is popped, very strong cig, gets me buzzed all the time.

Marlboro W-BURST

Tier: A

Decent menthol if you pop both of the capsules, the tobacco is not the greatest though and its too light for me.

Lucky Strike Wild

Tier: A

Good amount of nicotine and decent menthol when you pop the blue capsule. I'm not a huge fan of the purple capsule though.

Sobranie Cocktail

Tier: A

Great packaging and great looking cigs. Very smooth taste, nice flavor and burns for a long time, but a bit light for me.

Karelia Slim Menthol

Tier: B

The menthol is weak and I just don't really enjoy slim cigarettes.

Kool Boost

Tier: B

Weak menthol and the body is just so small.

Lucky Strike Chillberry

Tier: B

I'm not a huge fan of the pop capsule in it, but otherwise its a good cig.

L&M Forward

Tier: B

A light cigarette with a small menthol kick. This is one of those cigarettes that bypasses the menthol ban in the EU. Its a bit light for me.

Lucky Strike Blue

Tier: B

Doesn't taste like much, but its a strong cigarette.

Marlboro Red 100's

Tier: B

Not the best taste, slightly chemical, but it burns for a long time and it gets you buzzed.

Marlboro Gold 100's

Tier: C

Very light and tastes like paper.

Marlboro Red

Tier: D

Way more chemical than the 100's variant, its a strong cigarette but I can't really stand the taste.

Winston XSpression Long

Tier: F

Horrible bitter taste. This cigarette supposed to have menthol but I didn't really taste it. At least the firm filter is nice I guess but I would never try this again.
